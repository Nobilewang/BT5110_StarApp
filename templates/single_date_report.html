Check The Average Revenue Over Time Span

    This function is used to check the average revenue of the retail company in a single date, all or selected stores, and all or selected products:

  • Average revenue of all stores with all products in a selected single date (i.e, "2017-01-02")
  • Average revenue of all stores with selected products (i.e, P0241) in a selected single date (i.e, "2017-01-02")
  • Average revenue of the selected store (i.e, S0050) with selected products (i.e, P0241) in a selected single date (i.e, "2017-01-02")
  • Average revenue of the selected store (i.e, S0050) with all products in selected store in a selected single date (i.e, "2017-01-02")

    • ***None of above four selection method are supported, Nothing will output***
